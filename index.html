Hi, I'm Tahir Aduragba

I'm a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science, Durham University. I work with Professor Alexandra Cristea in the Innovative Computing Group.

My research interest is in deep learning, natural language processing and data science. Specifically, I'm interested in the prediction of infectious disease spread on social media.

I'm also the founder of SITASYS Nigeria where I provide Odoo and software consulting.

In 2011, I received my BSc degree in Computer Science (with first class) from Brunel University London. I got my MSc degree in Information Systems from the Alliance Manchester Business School in 2013. Details are in my CV
